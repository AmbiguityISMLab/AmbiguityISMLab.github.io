Hunt an Ambiguous Tweet which meets the following Qualifying Conditions:

Instructions:

  1. Select an ambiguous tweet. An ambiguous tweet is open to more than one different interpretation or meaning.
  2. Use the URL: http://ambiguity.ismlab.usf.edu to verify the parse trees and their probabilities.
  3. Paste the tweet in the textbox and click on Get Parse Trees. Note the Probability 1 and 2 shown below the trees.
  4. The tweet must be from a verified account (meaning the account has a blue verified badge on Twitter)
  5. The language for the tweets is restricted to English and the submission needs to be Single Sentence.
  6. You can provide at most two tweets from the same account.
  7. The tweets selected should be complete sentences which are grammatically correct. Tweets which are sentence fragments or phrases are not accepted.
  8. Any tweets wherein the content are obscene, abusive and defamatory will be rejected.
  9. You must provide at least two different interpretations of the Tweet.
  10. You are expected to score each Interpretation on a scale of 1-10. [1=Least Likely and 10=Most Likely]
  11. The tweet interpretation should not be ambiguous solely based on context.
  12. Below are examples of tweets that would be accepted: 
  • The sheriff caught the man with a gun.

Interpretations:

1. The sheriff used the gun to catch the man.

 2.  The sheriff caught the man carrying a gun.

  • The mayor asked the police to stop drinking. 

 Interpretations:

1. The mayor asked the police to arrest drunks.

2. The mayor asked the police to be sober on the job.

  • I still don't get micellar water. 

 Interpretations:

1. I still don't understand the meaning of micellar water.

2. I still don't receive micellar water.

       11. Below are examples of tweets that would be rejected:

  • Two scoop kinda day

Interpretations:

                         1.Two scoops of protein powder?

                        2.Two scoops of ice cream

  • KOREAN WAR TO END! The United States, and all of its GREAT people should be very proud of what is now taking place in Korea!

Interpretations:

                        1.NATO, USA, South Korea, etc will beat North Korea in a conventional or nuclear war and end the conflict

                       2.NATO, USA, South Korea, etc will sign a treaty ending the Korean War with North Korea.

  • Tesla team celebrates Pride Month

Interpretations:

                        1. The Tesla team is celebrating the LGBTQ Pride Month.

                       Pride Month is a well-known event and hence the only interpretion could be what is presented above.
